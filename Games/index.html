Space Shooter

Instructions:
click on the PLAY text for starting the game.
Navigation: use 'w','a','s','d' or up,down,right,left arrows for navigating the ship.
shooting: use LMB(left mouse button) for shooting.
Restart: press 'R' to restart the game.

Objective: try to score maximum score without getting collided by the asteroids.

This is the first game I could finish successfully from end to end.
I followed few tutorial to finishing this game.
I learnt more wide variety of concepts for developing a game.

I think this was a good start, I got familiar with many jargons of game development.
I found different resources online, which might be useful for future game development.

Not Medicine ball

Instructions:
Navigation:use 'w','a','s','d' or up,down,right,left arrows to roll the ball.
Restart: press R to restart. Objective: Try to collect the cubes without falling into the abyss.
Hint: The ball holds the momentum, even when you stop navigating.
Try to compensate the force by pressing the navigation button opposite to that of the movement of the ball.

I got the basic Idea for this game from one of the tutorials, when I was doing the previous game.
I wanted to put whatever new skills I learnt for test.
I should say I'm pretty much happy with the results.
